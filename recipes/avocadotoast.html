Avocado Toast Recipe

Description

Avocado toast is the ultimate healthy breakfast. It’s quick, nutritious, and endlessly customizable.


Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: poached egg, chili flakes, feta cheese

Steps

  1. Toast bread slices to your liking.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread mashed avocado on toast.
  4. Add desired toppings and serve immediately.
