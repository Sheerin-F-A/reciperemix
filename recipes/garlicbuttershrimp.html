Garlic Butter Shrimp Recipe

Description

This garlic butter shrimp is quick, easy, and absolutely delicious. It's perfect for a busy weeknight dinner.


Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add shrimp, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  4. Drizzle with lemon juice and garnish with parsley before serving.
